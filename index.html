About

Iraq Reborn is a cinematic 2026 Hearts of Iron IV submod expanding Iraq with modern security, intelligence, military, political, diplomatic, and national development content.

INSS Expansion

  • Multi level Iraqi National Security Service system
  • INSS decision system
  • Security and intelligence national ideas
  • Internal stability and state authority mechanics
  • Counter intelligence and national protection themes

ICTS Expansion

  • ICTS battalion and brigade development system
  • Special operations decision system
  • Counter terrorism modernization ideas
  • Elite unit readiness and deployment themes
  • Military security and rapid response content

Focus Tree Content

  • 30+ new Iraq 2026 national focuses
  • Foreign policy content around the United States
  • Regional diplomacy and strategic alignment branches
  • Security, economy, military, and intelligence paths
  • Beta foundation for future political expansions

Ideas and Decisions

  • Large collection of national spirits
  • Security, military, economic, and diplomatic ideas
  • Decision based modernization systems
  • US foreign policy influence and cooperation content
  • More ideas and events planned for future versions
