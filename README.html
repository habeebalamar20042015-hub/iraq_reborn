About The Mod

Iraq Reborn is a highly ambitious beta submod for Hearts of Iron IV, expanding Iraq in 2026 with new security, intelligence, military, political, diplomatic, economic, focus tree, idea, and event content.

Core Features

  • Expanded Iraqi National Security Service content.
  • Expanded Iraqi Counter Terrorism Service content.
  • New Iraq 2026 national focus tree paths.
  • New ideas, spirits, decisions, and event chains.
  • Modern Iraq rebuilding, security, and power projection themes.
  • Beta foundation for future political and military branches.

  • MD Revelations 2026
